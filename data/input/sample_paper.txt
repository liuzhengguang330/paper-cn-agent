Title: Decarbonizing Buildings Through Heat Pump Deployment

Abstract
Buildings account for a significant share of global carbon emissions. This paper evaluates how heat pump deployment and building envelope retrofits can reduce emissions while improving energy efficiency.

1. Introduction
Net zero pathways require deep retrofits in both residential and commercial buildings.

2. Results
A 35% reduction in carbon emissions was observed in the pilot region between 2020 and 2024.

3. Conclusion
Heat pump adoption combined with envelope upgrades is a cost-effective strategy.
